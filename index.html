Pass the Minions

About Pass the Minions

Pass the Minions takes inspiration from Winning Moves Games' popular title Pass The Pigs. The object is to be the first to score 100 points using "minions" as dice.

How to Play

On Your Turn

Roll both Minions. Points are scored according to the position of the Minions. After rolling, you must decide whether to stop rolling and bank your score, or to keep rolling to try and add points to your score. Be careful though, if you roll one "Silly" Minion and one "Bored" Minion, you lose all of your points accumulated in this turn and your turn ends. (See Scoring.)

Winning the Game

The first player to score 100 points wins the game.

Scoring

One Silly minion and one Bored minion. You score 0 points for this turn. This ends your turn.

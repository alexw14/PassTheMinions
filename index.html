Pass the Minions

About Pass the Minions

Pass the Minions takes inspiration from Winning Moves Games' popular title Pass The Pigs. The object of the game is to be the first player to score 100 points using "Minions" as dice.

How to Play

On Your Turn

Roll both Minions. Points are scored according to the position of the Minions. After rolling, you must decide whether to stop rolling and bank your score, or to keep rolling to try and add points to your score. Be careful though, if you roll one Silly Minion and one Bored Minion, you lose all of your points accumulated in this turn and your turn ends. (See Scoring.)

Winning the Game

The first player to score 100 points wins the game.

Controls

Using mouse: Click and hold down the Roll button to spin the Minions. Release the Roll button to roll the Minions and gain points. Click the Bank button to save the points accumulated in this turn.

Using keyboard: Hold down the spacebar to spin the Minions. Release the spacebar to roll the Minions and gain points. Press the 'B' key to save the points accumulated in this turn.

Scoring

One Silly minion and one Bored minion. You score 0 points for this turn. This ends your turn. Pass the Minions!
